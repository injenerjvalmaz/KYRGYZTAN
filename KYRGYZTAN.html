Kyrgyzstan is our homeland, and despite its small size in Central Asia, it is good to be unique.
To maintain your uniqueness, it is important to have your
own individuality and differences from other countries. In my opinion, in 10 years Kyrgyzstan will change for the better, because in the last two years, since Sadyr Japar became president,
Kyrgyzstan has been improving for the better. If our entrepreneurs support our youth,
our students, there will be a colossal change for the better.
If people continue to hate each other and do not understand, then, naturally, Kyrgyzstan
will go for the worse.
